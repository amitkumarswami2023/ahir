About Ahir College of Nursing

The Ahir College Society, known for its unwavering service in the fields of education and sports, with a legacy of integrity spanning generations, is now establishing a new institute: Ahir College of Nursing.
The Society brings decades of experience in managing reputable institutions. Among them, Ahir College, Rewari stands as a symbol of educational empowerment, especially for students from South Haryana—a region once considered educationally backward. For years, Ahir College was the sole centre of higher education in the area, carrying forward a proud legacy of inclusivity and opportunity.
Building on this foundation, Ahir College of Nursing will initially offer two programs: B.Sc. Nursing and General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)—both approved by the competent authorities.
The college features a lush green campus, a serene environment, and state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and comprehensive sports facilities. Our qualified, experienced, and compassionate faculty are committed to delivering high-quality education and hands-on training, equipping students with the knowledge, skills, ethics, and empathy required to succeed in today’s healthcare environment.
With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, clinical practice, and community service, Ahir College of Nursing aims to produce competent and compassionate healthcare professionals ready to make a meaningful impact in the healthcare sector.

Our Mission:

  • Deliver rigorous academic and clinical training that equips students with the competence, confidence, and compassion needed in modern healthcare environments.
  • Inculcate ethical, patient-centred care through a curriculum rooted in empathy, cultural sensitivity, and evidence-based nursing practices.
  • Empower future nursing leaders with strong professional values, critical thinking skills, and a lifelong commitment to service and continuous learning.

Compassion

Inspire a generation of nurses who lead with empathy, uphold dignity, and deliver patient care that truly heals.

Leadership

Equip students with the skills, confidence, and ethical grounding to lead in hospitals, communities, and evolving global health systems.
